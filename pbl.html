What is strength and stamina in workout?

Strength: As a sport definition, it’s the ability to carry out work against a resistance. Training can improve your strength like lifting weights or otherwise.

Stamina is defined as “the ability to sustain prolonged physical or mental effort,” according to Oxford Dictionary. With a good stamina, you can run faster for long distance. The better stamina, the more efficient you become at just about everything, mentally and physically.
