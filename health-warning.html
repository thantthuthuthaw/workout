Workout injuries

Workout injuries and prevention
A workout injury can happen to anyone, no matter experience or fitness level. Even walking can cause the injury.

What are the types of injuries?

  • Shoulder injury
  • Knee injuries
  • Muscle pull and strain

How can we prevent the workout injuries?
The ways are simple. You just need to follow it.

  1. Warm-up

    2. Every workout should begin with a warm up and end with the cool down period. Making a warm up helps your body to get ready for exercise. It increase your heart rate and loosens your muscles and joints.

  2. Cool-down

    3. A cool-down is doing after workout and it’s important too because it’s slowly bring your heart rate back to normal.

  3. Stretch

    4. Do stretching before and after you workout. This will help increase flexibility. Some research says, “To prevent injuries, the best way is to stretch after your warm-up and cool-down.

  4. Take rest

    5. Take 1 to 2 days off a week to rest. Taking rest is important to grow your muscles and recharge your energy.

  5. Fuel your body

    6. Drink plenty of water before, during, and after you workout. Eat a small meal or snack every 2 to 3 hours to keep the fuel for your body. Eat a lot of protein too.

Is it important to eat before workout?
Following the nutrients diet plan is the best way to achieve your goal but you need to put fuel for your workout. If you do not eat enough in general, before exercising, you might have nutrient deficiencies, muscle cramps or irregular heartbeat. Without eating before exercising will take your valuable energy and have less stamina. Not saying to eat a lot. Suggest: one egg or two is fine.
