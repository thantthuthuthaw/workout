Each exercise 30 times at least.

Scissor Skier

Tips:
1. Stand tall with your feet together, arms by your sides, and your palms facing your hips.
2. Jump up, bring your right leg forward, your left leg back, and reach your left arm up and your right arm back.
3. Jump again and switch your legs and arms.
4. Continue switching until the set is complete.

High Knees

Tips:
1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Lift up your left knee to your chest.
2. Switch to lift your right knee to your chest. Continue the movement, alternating legs and moving at a sprinting or running pace.

Rotate Jump Squat

Tips:
1. Start in squat position, jump up as high as possible, and then rotate to the land facing the opposite way.
2. Repeat.

Knees Hug

Tips:
1. Sit down on the mat with your knees bent, your hands hugging your knees, and lift your feet off the floor.
2. Open your arms, extend your legs to a 45-degree angle, and lean back.
3. Lift your torso, bend your knees, and return to the starting position.
4. Repeat.

Plank With Alternate Arm & Leg Raise

Tips:
1. Come into plank position with your arms and legs straight and your shoulders above your wrists.
2. With control, raise your right arm up as you simultaneously raise your left leg up off of the ground. Be sure not to rotate at your hip or upper body. Hold for one second.
3. With control, lower your right arm and left leg back to the starting position. That counts as one rep. Repeat with the left arm and right leg.

Ski Abs

Tips:
1. Start with your body in a straight line, your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart and feet together.
2. Bend your knees and jump, bringing both feet to the front and slightly to the left side.
3. Jump again to return to the starting position and then repeat on the right side.
4. Continue alternating sides until the set is complete.

Russian Twist

Tips:
1. Sit with your knees bent out in front of you, feet flexed, and heels on the floor.
2. Hold your hands in front of your chest and lean your torso back until you feel your abdominal muscles engage.
3. Slowly twist your torso from right to left. Remember to keep your core tight (and breathe!) throughout.

Single Leg Bridge | Please do the both sides.

Tips:
1. Lie on your back with your arms by your sides, knees bent, and feet flat on the floor.
2. Raise one leg and lift your hips as high as you can.
3. Lower your hips, repeat, and then switch legs.

Up & Down Plank

Tips:
1. Start in a plank position, with your wrists under your shoulders and your feet hip-width apart.
2. Bend your left arm, place your left elbow on the mat and then bend your right arm and place your right elbow on the mat.
3. Place your left hand on the mat, straighten your left arm and then place your right hand on the mat and straighten your right arm.
4. Switch sides and repeat this up and down movement until the set is complete.

Push Up

Tips:
1. With your legs extended back, place the hands below the shoulders, slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
2. Start bending your elbows and lower your chest until itâ€™s just above the floor.
3. Push back to the starting position. A 1-second push, 1-second pause, 2-second down count is ideal.
4. Repeat.

Plank

Tips:
1. Begin on all fours, placing your hands on the ground directly beneath your shoulders.
2. Next, step your feet behind you, grounding down into your toes to form a straight line from head to toe.
3. Engage your legs, glutes, and core as you maintain a neutral neck and spine.
4. Hold for 50 seconds with good form.

Do this for 3 routines at least.

After one routine, rest 1 minute.
